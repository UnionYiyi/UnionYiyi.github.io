Cloud-like Cotton

Born from the cotton fields of China, we choose only the gentlest touch. Bouncy, breathable, never too slippery or stiff — like sleeping inside a cloud.

We don’t choose cotton for luxury, but for memory. For the way it hugs, folds, and breathes — like a grandmother’s quilt, or a safe Sunday morning.
