Silk

Softness you can’t explain. Light you want to stay in.

Our silk doesn’t whisper — it lingers. It traces the shape of stillness, of skin, of longing. It’s cool at first touch, then warm with time — just like intimacy.

Woven from pure mulberry silk, it moves with you and remembers you. It slides where cotton rests, listens where linen breathes.

This isn’t the silk of display — it’s silk made for touch. For sleep, yes, but also for closeness. For softness that wakes something in you.

Some fabrics comfort. Silk awakens.
